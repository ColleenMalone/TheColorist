Who We Are

The Colorist was founded in the summer of 2007 by three best friends who just completed their cosmetology degrees. Each one of these ladies brought a different skill to the table making The Colorist a one stop shop for all of their clients' beauty needs. The Colorist prides itself on high quality products and fair pricing. Over the past 15 years, The Colorist has grown and the three friends are now talking about opening up a second location.
